MY STORY

Building Enterprise Software, One Feature at a Time

Hi! I'm Pratik Nere — a Software Engineer from Pune with 2.8+ years of experience building enterprise hospitality applications at Cybage Software. I specialize in the Hotel Channel Management domain, working on OTA integrations, reservation systems, and scalable microservices.

My stack spans the full pipeline: Angular 20 on the frontend with Standalone Components, Signals, and Reactive Forms; Spring Boot 3.x on the backend with microservices, Spring Security, and JPA; event-driven architecture with Apache Kafka; and multi-database expertise across Oracle, PostgreSQL, Couchbase, and Elasticsearch.

I actively use GitHub Copilot and AI tooling to ship faster without compromising on quality. Outside work, I'm a gym enthusiast, bike rider, and side-project builder — currently working on StayConnect and FitedIn.
